PROJECT | 06

Media & communication science study

Degree project | 2020

Google is the world's largest search engine and a medium that has an enormous power to be able to influence human perception and its view of reality through the internet. The information and knowledge that people take a part of through Google, somehow becomes a matter of course and is seen as the true and the normal. If only a certain group of people are allowed to be included on Google, it becomes a problem.

The purpose of the study is to investigate whether Google Images contributes to the promotion of the whiteness norm and gender stereotypes.
